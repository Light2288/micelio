Privacy Policy

I Collect No Personal Information From This Application

I do not collect, use, save, or have access to any of your personal data recorded in Micelio. Mushroom positions and relative information saved in the app are stored only on your device. You might also be asked to allow notifications from the app, but this is only necessary to receive geolocalised notifications when you are near to one of your mushroom positions; this information is not processed outside the app.
